Justin Bieber

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer and songwriter who rose to fame as a teenager with his debut single "One Time" in 2009. Known for hits like "Baby," "Love Yourself," and "Peaches," he has become one of the best-selling music artists in the world, recognized for his pop and R&B sound, as well as his influence on modern music culture.
